Teaching Pedagogy

With diverse practice and research activities, my teaching pedagogy stems from a trans-multi-disciplinary focus around the built environment. Teaching is a lifelong passion that enables constant learning through active sharing.

"The co-learners range from senior professionals and university graduates to school children, emphasizing collective action and public interest."
