Purpose

The core mission driving my endeavors.

"Hope for a better tomorrow and a wish to be of some Help towards it keeps me going."

My practice, research, and teaching converge towards a life purpose where meanings are discovered in extracurricular activities—including observing people and embracing stillness. Debates and discussions on water and women, politics and people, science and spirituality, fitness and films are enjoyable collective works, alongside the solo processes of sketching, scribbling, cooking, and cleaning.

A key area that has grown with age is mentoring and counselling on environmental, emotional, and educational matters. It is incredibly enriching to hear from unfamiliar individuals who quickly become close, valued connections.

One non-negotiable core value in my life is freedom. True divinity lies in the freedom of thinking, expressing, and (not) doing. With the freedom of decisions and actions comes deep responsibility.

Responsible Citizen Fellowship (RCF)

To learn and share the values of freedom as well as responsibility, a small grant program namely, 'Responsible Citizen Fellowship, RCF' is arranged from endowments through a not-for-profit initiative, eco development and research cell (EDRC), of the parent consultancy firm, Environmental Design Consultants Ahmedabad (EDC).
