Response

Reactions and impacts in the architectural space.

Expressing concerns about the events happening both within and outside related to living and working is an integral part of being a responsible citizen. I believe in active and sensitive citizenship, and thus actively engage and respond to key issues. If more citizens register their opinions, the functioning of our social-ecological system and democratic values can be restored.

My focus areas are embedded in humanity and biodiversity, bridging environment and economy, government and governance, people and politics, and education and scholarship.
